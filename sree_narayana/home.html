About Organization

Sree Narayana Engineering Works is a precision machining company located in Thirumullaivayal, Chennai, India. Established in 2020, we have become a trusted partner for engineering manufacturing solutions.

The facility is equipped with CNC, lathe, and drilling machines, enabling the machining of components such as water pump and oil pump cover plates, housings, pulleys, hubs, and end covers, strictly as per client specifications.

The company works with customer-provided materials including Cast Iron, Mild Steel (MS), EN8, EN1A, and other specified materials. A strong focus on accuracy, quality, and timely delivery has helped the organization build long-term relationships with its clients.

Sree Narayana Engineering Works is GST registered and holds a government sustainability certification, reflecting its commitment to responsible and efficient machining practices.

Our vision is to achieve independent raw material sourcing and manufacturing capabilities to further strengthen our market position.
